# Lenzy — The Vision

## What this is

**Lenzy is the central knowledge brain for the global eyewear industry.** Every brand, every product, every piece of creative, every person building in the space, every celebrity and influencer wearing eyewear — collected, structured, searchable, exportable, used by Lenskart's team daily to make faster decisions and find better hires.

Not a dashboard. Not a feed. A **knowledge layer** that the entire Lenskart team can query like Google but for "what's happening in eyewear right now."

## Why it exists

Lenskart is the world's largest eyewear retailer outside of EssilorLuxottica's portfolio. The team needs to:

1. **Move faster on creative.** When a trend pops in Korea, Lenskart's team should see it in 24 hours, not 4 weeks.
2. **Buy smarter.** Merchandising decisions today are made on gut feel. They should be made on real-time data from 3,500+ competitor catalogs.
3. **Hire from the industry.** The best eyewear designers, supply chain leads, and brand builders are scattered across 500+ companies. Lenzy maps them all.
4. **Spot trends before competitors.** A Vision-first pipeline that detects unbranded eyewear in any photo on the internet — micro-influencer wearing a frame today, in a Vogue spread next month.
5. **Cross-pollinate.** What's Warby doing in retail? What's Gentle Monster doing in store design? What's Caddis doing in pricing? All in one place.

## The unfair advantage

Three things make this defensible:

1. **Vision-first.** Most competitive intel tools rely on metadata (hashtags, captions). Lenzy uses Gemini Vision to extract eyewear attributes from any image — branded or not. This means we can detect trends from press photos, paparazzi shots, UGC, micro-influencers — months before they hit the trade press.

2. **Two-table polymorphic architecture.** Every piece of content (post, product, person, link, ad, news, reimagine) lives in one table tied to a brand. This means *every* future feature is just a new query, not a new schema migration. Built for compounding.

3. **Reimagine loop.** Spot a competitor's hit post → generate a Lenskart variant in 30 seconds → ship to social. Closes the loop from intel to action inside one tool.

## Three-year horizon

**Year 1:** Internal tool, Lenskart team only, 5,000 brands, 500k products, 10k people, 100k+ creative posts.

**Year 2:** Open read-only API to trusted partners (eyewear retailers, distributors, analysts). Add adjacent verticals — smart glasses, contact lenses, optical retail tech.

**Year 3:** Either a paid SaaS for the eyewear industry (subscription per seat) or absorbed deeper into Lenskart as a strategic data moat. Optional: spin out as a standalone intelligence company.
